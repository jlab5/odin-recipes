Rainbow Terrine Recipe

Made by:

Megumi Tadokoro
Soma Yukihira

Description

A terrine made with sevens colorful layers of various vegetables, resembling a rainbow. This dish offers a fun and delicious way to try different vegetable flavors. It also has a secret ingredient, allspice, which adds a comforting warmth, evoking a feeling of being on a tropical beach on a sunny day.
